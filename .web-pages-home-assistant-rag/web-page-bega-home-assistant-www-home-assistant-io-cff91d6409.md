Supported devices
BEGA is a member of the Works with Home Assistant partner program for their Zigbee products. BEGA is committed to making sure their products are up-to-date and ready to use in Home Assistant.
BEGA Zigbee devices work locally and integrate seamlessly with the Zigbee integration in Home Assistant (Zigbee stick required). As all connectivity is happening locally, status updates and controlling your devices happen instantly in Home Assistant.
Learn more about Zigbee in Home Assistant.
For the full list of 165 certified BEGA devices, see our filtered device list .
