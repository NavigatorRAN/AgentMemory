Configuration
The RAVEn family of devices from Rainforest Automation, Inc. includes the EMU-2 , a wireless device that reads energy usage information directly from your utility meter. This integration connects to a RAVEn-compatible device attached to the Home Assistant instance via USB to integrate instantaneous power demand, accumulated usage, and current unit pricing.
To add the Rainforest RAVEn hub to your Home Assistant instance, use this My button:
Manual configuration steps
If the above My button doesn’t work, you can also perform the following steps
manually:
Browse to your Home Assistant instance.
Go to Settings > Devices & services .
In the bottom right corner, select the
Add Integration button.
From the list, select Rainforest RAVEn .
Follow the instructions on screen to complete the setup.
