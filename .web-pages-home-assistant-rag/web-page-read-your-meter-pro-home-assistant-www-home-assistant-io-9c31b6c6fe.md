The Read Your Meter Pro integration Integrations connect and integrate Home Assistant with your devices, services, and more. [Learn more] connects with a Read Your Meter Pro account to get water meter reads into Home Assistant.
To add the Read Your Meter Pro hub to your Home Assistant instance, use this My button:
Manual configuration steps
If the above My button doesn’t work, you can also perform the following steps
manually:
Browse to your Home Assistant instance.
Go to Settings > Devices & services .
In the bottom right corner, select the
Add Integration button.
From the list, select Read Your Meter Pro .
Follow the instructions on screen to complete the setup.
