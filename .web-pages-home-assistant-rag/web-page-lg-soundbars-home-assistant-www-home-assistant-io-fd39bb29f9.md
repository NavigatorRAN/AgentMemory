The LG Soundbars integration Integrations connect and integrate Home Assistant with your devices, services, and more. [Learn more] allows you to control LG Soundbars from Home Assistant.
Supported devices:
Devices in the SK and SN range with Ethernet and/or Wi-Fi
To add the LG Soundbars device to your Home Assistant instance, use this My button:
Manual configuration steps
If the above My button doesn’t work, you can also perform the following steps
manually:
Browse to your Home Assistant instance.
Go to Settings > Devices & services .
In the bottom right corner, select the
Add Integration button.
From the list, select LG Soundbars .
Follow the instructions on screen to complete the setup.
These devices support mute, volume control, source selection and mode selection.
