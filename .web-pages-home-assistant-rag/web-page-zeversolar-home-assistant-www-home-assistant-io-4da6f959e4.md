You need a Zeversolar inverter connected to your solar panels and Wi-Fi. Furthermore, you need to

Manual configuration steps
If the above My button doesn’t work, you can also perform the following steps
manually:
Browse to your Home Assistant instance.
Go to Settings > Devices & services .
In the bottom right corner, select the
Add Integration button.
From the list, select Zeversolar .
Follow the instructions on screen to complete the setup.
When configured, the integration Integrations connect and integrate Home Assistant with your devices, services, and more. [Learn more] will create two sensors for each configured inverter:
Energy today, in kWh (Kilowatt-hour)
Current power, in W (Watts)
