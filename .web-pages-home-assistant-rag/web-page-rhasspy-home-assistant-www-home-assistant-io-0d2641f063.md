The Rhasspy integration Integrations connect and integrate Home Assistant with your devices, services, and more. [Learn more] allows you to use your Rhasspy voice assistant with your Home Assistant installation. Rhasspy is an open source, fully offline set of voice assistant services for many human languages.
To add the Rhasspy integration to your Home Assistant instance, use this My button:
Manual configuration steps
If the above My button doesn’t work, you can also perform the following steps
manually:
Browse to your Home Assistant instance.
Go to Settings > Devices & services .
In the bottom right corner, select the
Add Integration button.
From the list, select Rhasspy .
Follow the instructions on screen to complete the setup.
Rhasspy comes in two flavors. If you want to just try out, you can connect a microphone and speaker to the machine running Home Assistant and install Rhasspy Junior as an app for Home Assistant. Once installed, you can say any of these pre-defined sentences .
