Another best practice with Assist is to create an architecture of areas and floors in your home, since it will make it consistent and easy to understand for Assist.
Go to Settings > Areas, labels & zones .
Select Create area .
Select Create floor .
In the floor creation dialog, assign the related areas.
Next to the floor name, select the three dots menu.
Select Edit floor .
Assign your areas to this floor.
Go to Settings > Devices & services > Devices .
Select the device.
In the top bar of the device page, select the pencil icon.
Assign an area to the device.
