SwitchBee , is an innovation company making smart homes more accessible and affordable to any household environment.
There is currently support for the following device types:
Switch
Timed Power Switch (Boiler)
Group Switch
Timed Switch
Shutter
Somfy Shutter
Light (Dimmer)
Scenario
Thermostat
Supported devices will be discovered after the SwitchBee integration is configured.
To add the SwitchBee hub to your Home Assistant instance, use this My button:
Manual configuration steps
If the above My button doesn’t work, you can also perform the following steps
manually:
Browse to your Home Assistant instance.
Go to Settings > Devices & services .
In the bottom right corner, select the
Add Integration button.
From the list, select SwitchBee .
Follow the instructions on screen to complete the setup.
