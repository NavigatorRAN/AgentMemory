Integrates Ruuvi ’s RuuviTag and Ruuvi Air BLE devices into Home Assistant.
To add the Ruuvi BLE device to your Home Assistant instance, use this My button:
Manual configuration steps
If the above My button doesn’t work, you can also perform the following steps
manually:
Browse to your Home Assistant instance.
Go to Settings > Devices & services .
In the bottom right corner, select the
Add Integration button.
From the list, select Ruuvi BLE .
Follow the instructions on screen to complete the setup.
The Ruuvi BLE integration will automatically discover supported devices once the Bluetooth integration is enabled and functional.
If your Ruuvi devices are not discovered, please make sure to update them to the latest firmware .
RuuviTag - Environmental sensor tags
RuuviTag Pro - Heavy-duty environmental sensor tags
Ruuvi Air - Air quality monitors (since Home Assistant 2025.11)
