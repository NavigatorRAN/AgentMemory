The HomeLink integration Integrations connect and integrate Home Assistant with your devices, services, and more. [Learn more] allows you to integrate your HomeLink devices in Home Assistant. The HomeLink app allows HomeLink owners to trigger smart home routines in a variety of cloud-based home automation platforms from the comfort of their vehicle. When you arrive home, one HomeLink button press can adjust the thermostat, turn on lights, disarm the security system, unlock the door, and begin playing your favorite music.

Manual configuration steps
If the above My button doesn’t work, you can also perform the following steps
manually:
Browse to your Home Assistant instance.
Go to Settings > Devices & services .
In the bottom right corner, select the
Add Integration button.
From the list, select HomeLink .
Follow the instructions on screen to complete the setup.
Email

This integration follows standard integration removal.
Go to Settings > Devices & services and select the integration card.
From the list of devices, select the integration instance you want to remove.
Next to the entry, select the three dots menu. Then, select Delete .
