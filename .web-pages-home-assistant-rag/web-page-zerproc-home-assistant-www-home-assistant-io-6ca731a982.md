Manual configuration steps
If the above My button doesn’t work, you can also perform the following steps
manually:
Browse to your Home Assistant instance.
Go to Settings > Devices & services .
In the bottom right corner, select the
Add Integration button.
From the list, select Zerproc .
Follow the instructions on screen to complete the setup.
The integration Integrations connect and integrate Home Assistant with your devices, services, and more. [Learn more] will scan for nearby devices, and is completed if any are found. No additional configuration is required. The integration will perform a BLE scan every 60 seconds to search for new devices.
