Integrate KMtronic devices into Home Assistant. KMtronic offers a large range of IP-connected relays that can be controlled over your local area network. This integration maps each of the relays as a separate device, enabling you to control various areas of your home (e.g. using each relay to control different garden sprinkler areas, or controlling the electrical distribution of your house from a central board).
To add the KMtronic device to your Home Assistant instance, use this My button:
Manual configuration steps
If the above My button doesn’t work, you can also perform the following steps
manually:
Browse to your Home Assistant instance.
Go to Settings > Devices & services .
In the bottom right corner, select the
Add Integration button.
From the list, select KMtronic .
Follow the instructions on screen to complete the setup.
