Heatit is a member of the Works with Home Assistant partner program for their Z-Wave products. Heatit is committed to making sure their products are up-to-date and ready to use in Home Assistant.
Heatit Z-Wave devices work locally and integrate seamlessly with the Z-Wave integration in Home Assistant (Z-Wave stick required). As all connectivity is happening locally, status updates and controlling your devices happen instantly in Home Assistant.
Learn more about Z-Wave in Home Assistant.
