Configuration
The Brunt Blind Engine integration Integrations connect and integrate Home Assistant with your devices, services, and more. [Learn more] allows one to control Blind Engines by Brunt . To use it, you need a Brunt App Account. All Brunt Blind devices registered to your account are automatically added to your Home Assistant with the names given them through the Brunt app.
Warning
This integration is not affiliated with Brunt and retrieves data from the endpoints of the mobile application. Use at your own risk.
To add the Brunt Blind Engine hub to your Home Assistant instance, use this My button:
Manual configuration steps
If the above My button doesn’t work, you can also perform the following steps
manually:
Browse to your Home Assistant instance.
Go to Settings > Devices & services .
In the bottom right corner, select the
Add Integration button.
From the list, select Brunt Blind Engine .
Follow the instructions on screen to complete the setup.
