The Local to-do list integration Integrations connect and integrate Home Assistant with your devices, services, and more. [Learn more] allows you to create to-do lists in Home Assistant.
To-do lists are shown on the To-do list dashboard for tracking items and whether
or not they have been completed.
See the To-do list integration for additional details
about to-do list entities.
To add the Local to-do integration to your Home Assistant instance, use this My button:
Manual configuration steps
If the above My button doesn’t work, you can also perform the following steps
manually:
Browse to your Home Assistant instance.
Go to Settings > Devices & services .
In the bottom right corner, select the
Add Integration button.
From the list, select Local to-do .
Follow the instructions on screen to complete the setup.
