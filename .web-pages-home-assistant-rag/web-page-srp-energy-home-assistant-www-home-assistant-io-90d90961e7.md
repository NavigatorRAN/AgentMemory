The SRP Energy integration Integrations connect and integrate Home Assistant with your devices, services, and more. [Learn more] shows information from SRP hourly energy usage report for their customers.
You need a username, password, and account ID which you can create at SRP .
To add the SRP Energy service to your Home Assistant instance, use this My button:
Manual configuration steps
If the above My button doesn’t work, you can also perform the following steps
manually:
Browse to your Home Assistant instance.
Go to Settings > Devices & services .
In the bottom right corner, select the
Add Integration button.
From the list, select SRP Energy .
Follow the instructions on screen to complete the setup.
