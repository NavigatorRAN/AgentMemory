The ProgettiHWSW Automation integration Integrations connect and integrate Home Assistant with your devices, services, and more. [Learn more] brings the automation experience with ProgettiHWSW boards to Home Assistant.
To add the ProgettiHWSW Automation device to your Home Assistant instance, use this My button:
Manual configuration steps
If the above My button doesn’t work, you can also perform the following steps
manually:
Browse to your Home Assistant instance.
Go to Settings > Devices & services .
In the bottom right corner, select the
Add Integration button.
From the list, select ProgettiHWSW Automation .
Follow the instructions on screen to complete the setup.
The progettihwsw binary sensor platform allows you to read the input values of a ProgettiHWSW Board .
The input numbers for sensors are shown on the board’s enclosure and PCB.
The progettihwsw switch platform allows you to control relays of a ProgettiHWSW Board .
For more information about the boards, visit our website .
There are no known errors with this integration yet. Feel free to submit them to us.
