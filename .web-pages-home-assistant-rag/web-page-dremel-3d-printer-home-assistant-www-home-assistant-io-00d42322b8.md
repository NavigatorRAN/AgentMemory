The Dremel 3D Printer integration Integrations connect and integrate Home Assistant with your devices, services, and more. [Learn more] allows you to monitor your Dremel 3D printer and its progress with your Home Assistant installation. This integration is currently limited to the 3D20, 3D40 and 3D45.

Manual configuration steps
If the above My button doesn’t work, you can also perform the following steps
manually:
Browse to your Home Assistant instance.
Go to Settings > Devices & services .
In the bottom right corner, select the
Add Integration button.
From the list, select Dremel 3D Printer .
Follow the instructions on screen to complete the setup.
