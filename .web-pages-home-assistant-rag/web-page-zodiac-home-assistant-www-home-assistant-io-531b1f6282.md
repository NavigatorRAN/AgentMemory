The Zodiac integration Integrations connect and integrate Home Assistant with your devices, services, and more. [Learn more] tracks the current zodiac sign.
To add the Zodiac integration to your Home Assistant instance, use this My button:
Manual configuration steps
If the above My button doesn’t work, you can also perform the following steps
manually:
Browse to your Home Assistant instance.
Go to Settings > Devices & services .
In the bottom right corner, select the
Add Integration button.
From the list, select Zodiac .
Follow the instructions on screen to complete the setup.
This integration offers a sensor that will return one of the following states:
aries , taurus , gemini , cancer , leo , virgo , libra , scorpio , sagittarius , capricorn , aquarius , pisces .
The sensor also exposes the following attributes:
element : fire , air , earth , water
modality : cardinal , fixed , mutable
