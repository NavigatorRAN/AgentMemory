The YouLess integration Integrations connect and integrate Home Assistant with your devices, services, and more. [Learn more] for Home Assistant allows you to read the meter values from sensors created by YouLess .
The integration Integrations connect and integrate Home Assistant with your devices, services, and more. [Learn more] is tested and verified for the following devices from YouLess:
LS110
LS120 running PVOutput firmware
LS120 running Enologic firmware
To add the YouLess device to your Home Assistant instance, use this My button:
Manual configuration steps
If the above My button doesn’t work, you can also perform the following steps
manually:
Browse to your Home Assistant instance.
Go to Settings > Devices & services .
In the bottom right corner, select the
Add Integration button.
From the list, select YouLess .
Follow the instructions on screen to complete the setup.
The integration Integrations connect and integrate Home Assistant with your devices, services, and more. [Learn more] will create sensors for you to display:
The current power usage
The gas meter
The water meter
The current meter value (including high and low)
The delivery meter value, for Solar power
